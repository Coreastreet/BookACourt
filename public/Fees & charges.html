Cancellation Fees

  • Regular bookings are deemed hires of 8 or more consecutive weeks/booking. Bookings cancelled inside the minimum 8 week requirement will be charged the difference for Casual Booking price.
  • Regular hires during School Holiday periods are deemed 3 or more consecutive days
  • Any changes to a regular booking will require 72 hours prior notice to receive a credit or transfer, and you must email bookings.miisc@cbcity.nsw.gov.au 
  • Cancellations to a booking less than 48 hours' notice will receive a 50% credit or refund
  • Cancellations to a booking less than 24 hours' notice will not receive a credit, refund or booking transfer
  • Cancellations to remaining long term bookings must give at least 4 weeks' notice or be charged for the full 4 week notice period.

Great facilities for schools 

Whether you run a competition on our courts, hire for school sport, or participate in group fitness classes, we can accommodate and modify the needs for every school.

We are confident, with the highest calibre of staff and facilities, we can deliver a great service to your school. 

Indoor courts can accommodate up to 25 students per court. This can vary depending on what sport you wish to play.  High Low F​itness can accommodate up to 60 children for a 45 minute group fitness session. Combining the two, the centre itself can accommodate more than 100 students per hour. ​
